Booking information

How booking, payment, waitlists, ballots and parking work.

Paid places

Anyone can book a paid place, including Sew Positive volunteers. Current Sew Positive charity participants cannot buy paid places — free places are available to you by ballot instead. You'll confirm this at checkout.

Free ballot places

Selected courses hold free places for participants, volunteers, trustees, ambassadors and peer-support leaders. Apply with your name, email and eligibility confirmation before the closing date; places are drawn at random and everyone is notified.

Waitlists

If a course is full you can join the waitlist. If a place frees up, we'll offer it to the next person in line by email with a time-limited link.

Parking permits

We have five visitor parking permits for applicable daytime courses. Request one during booking and we'll confirm availability. Venue access is currently via one flight of stairs — please tell us about any access needs when you book.

Paying by instalments

Longer, higher-value courses may be paid in instalments. Where available you'll see the option at checkout.
